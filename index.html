Dodge Survive done

A small top-down survival game in Python, plus a reinforcement-learning agent that learns to play it on its own. You move around an arena dodging obstacles that fly in from the edges; the longer you last, the harder it gets. I built the whole loop end to end — environment, reward, training — to actually understand RL. The agent learns purely from trial and error; it never watches a human play.
